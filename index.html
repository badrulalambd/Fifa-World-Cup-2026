Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need Before Match Day

Find quick answers to the most common questions about tickets, stadium entry, match schedules, and event guidelines for the FIFA World Cup 2026.

How can I purchase match tickets?

Tickets can be purchased through the official ticket portal. Availability varies depending on the match and tournament stage.

When should I arrive at the stadium?

Fans are encouraged to arrive at least two hours before kickoff to complete security screening and enjoy pre-match activities.

Can I transfer my ticket to another person?

Ticket transfer options may be available depending on the ticket type and tournament regulations.

Will match schedules change?

Match dates and kickoff times may be updated due to operational or broadcasting requirements. Always check the latest schedule.

Are food and beverages available inside the stadium?

Yes. Every stadium offers a variety of food and beverage options, including local specialties and international favorites.

What items are prohibited inside the venue?

Large bags, weapons, fireworks, drones, and other restricted items are not permitted inside the stadium.
